Isha Upa Yoga - Free Practices (open to all)

Upa Yoga is a simple yet powerful system of exercise that activates the joints, muscles, and energy system. Based on a sophisticated understanding of the body's mechanics, Upa Yoga dispels inertia in the body's energy and brings ease to the whole system.

Benifits

  • Strengthens and stabilizes the spine, can relieve back pain, stress & anxiety.
  • Can relieve from chronic ailments and improves overall health.
  • Does not require any fitness level or previous exposure to yoga.
  • Practices taught can be done anywhere between 5 to 21 minutes.
Date: 18th September 2016, 6:45 pm - 8:00 pm

{{> drop-shadow}} {{> drop-shadow }} Bhava Spandana in Australia (Oct 8 - 11, 2016)

Bhava Spandana Program (BSP) is a 4-day, 3-night, residential program offered to those who have been initiated into Shambhavi Mahamudra. This advanced meditation program is designed by Sadhguru to provide an opportunity to experience higher levels of consciousness, beyond the limitations of body and mind. BSP offers the experience of a world of limitless love and joy.

For more details, visit ishayoga.org.au/bhava-spandana
